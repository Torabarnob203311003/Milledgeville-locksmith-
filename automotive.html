Ignition Repair Service

One of the most common automotive problems we see around Central Georgia is ignition wear. A lot of ignition problems begin slowly over time.

Many people immediately assume the entire ignition needs replacement. In many situations, proper repair may still be possible before replacing the full ignition assembly. We focus on properly inspecting the problem first and avoiding unnecessary replacement whenever possible.

Older Honda, Toyota, Ford, GM, and Dodge vehicles commonly develop ignition wear after years of daily use.

Water-Damaged Smart Keys

Around Lake Sinclair, water-damaged smart keys are a common emergency. Learn what to do if your smart key gets wet to minimize damage.

💧 If Your Smart Key Gets Wet A lot of people continue pressing the buttons repeatedly hoping the remote will suddenly work again. Unfortunately, that can sometimes create even more internal damage.

Steps to take immediately:
  • 1. Remove the emergency key
  • 2. Open the remote as soon as possible
  • 3. Remove the battery immediately
  • 4. Allow the remote to dry naturally
  • 5. Avoid direct heat whenever possible

The faster the battery is removed, the better the chance of reducing corrosion damage inside the remote.
