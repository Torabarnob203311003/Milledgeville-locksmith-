Storefront Locks

Storefront Lock Repair

Storefront doors experience heavy daily use. Over time, keys wear down, cylinders wear unevenly, hardware loosens, doors shift out of alignment, and locks become harder to operate.

⚠️ Signs of Wear to Watch For

  • Keys sticking
  • Difficulty locking the door
  • Loose handles
  • Doors failing to latch properly
  • Storefront locks jamming unexpectedly

In many situations, proper adjustment or repair may still solve the issue before complete replacement becomes necessary.

A lot of businesses around downtown Milledgeville still use older storefront locks and commercial cylinders that have been operating for many years without proper service.

Catching storefront lock problems early may help avoid larger hardware failures and unexpected business downtime.
